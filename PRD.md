# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Problem Statement
Siz is experiencing potential revenue loss due to inefficient or inaccurate session tracking of games played in her business.

## Pain Points
1. Potential loss in revenue from agents misrecording the number of games played to keep a larger share of profit.
2. Lack of accurate, clear metrics to show the exact number of games played each day.

## Solution
- Implement a daily record system for games played, including the number of sessions and other business metrics.

## Expected Results
- Owner: Improved cash flow, peace of mind, and increased revenue.
- System: Dashboard with full games played data across all business locations, including inventory.
- Data: Daily record of games played, including average expected income.

## Expected Outcome
A clear, tamper-proof record of games played each day for better analysis and improved cash flow.
