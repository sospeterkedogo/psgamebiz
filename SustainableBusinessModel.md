# Sustainable Business Model

## Revenue Protection
- Tamper-proof session tracking to prevent underreporting
- Accurate daily metrics for games played and revenue

## Owner Benefits
- More cash flow
- Peace of mind
- Data-driven decision making

## System Features
- Dashboard with full games played data across all business locations
- Inventory tracking
- Daily, weekly, and monthly reporting

## Data Integrity
- Immutable logs for session/game records
- Regular audits and alerts for anomalies

## Scalability
- Designed to support multiple locations
- Modular for future feature additions

## Sustainability
- Reduces manual errors and fraud
- Enables better forecasting and planning
- Supports business growth with reliable data
